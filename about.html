I am a cartographer and developer interested in telling the stories of communities through maps and technology. I have helped local governments and non-profits tell their stories through maps for print, interactive web maps, and websites.

Most of my professional experience has been working with county governments. I have also completed freelance projects for non-profits and web development companies.

Some projects I'm especially proud of are building a blogging site with my wife, redesigning the hiking trails map (pdf) for Camp Swatara, and creating the Cumberland County Residential Recycling & Disposal Map.

If you're interested in collaborating on a project, or have any questions, feel free to e-mail me at pnmcartography[at]gmail.com
